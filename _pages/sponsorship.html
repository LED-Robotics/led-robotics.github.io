Sponsor our team!

Supporting LED Robotics Team 6722 creates a powerful ripple effect that extends far beyond the competition field. Your sponsorship directly empowers our students to dive deep into real-world engineering challenges, developing crucial skills in robotics, programming, and mechanical design through the prestigious FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). But the impact reaches much further – our team serves as a catalyst for STEM education in our community, inspiring younger students through robotics demonstrations at local schools and community events. Your investment helps provide the essential tools, materials, and competition fees needed to keep our program running, while also supporting our community outreach initiatives that spark interest in technology and innovation. Through mentorship and hands-on experience, our students develop not just technical expertise, but also vital leadership, teamwork, and project management skills that prepare them for future careers in STEM fields. By partnering with LED Robotics Team 6722, you're not just supporting a robotics team – you're investing in the next generation of innovators and problem-solvers who will shape our community's future.
